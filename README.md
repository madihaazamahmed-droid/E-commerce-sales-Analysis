# E-commerce-sales-Analysis
 E‑Commerce Sales Analysis — Description E‑commerce Sales Analysis is the process of examining online sales data to understand customer behavior, product performance, and overall business growth. It helps businesses make data‑driven decisions by identifying trends, strengths, and areas for improvement.
Key Focus Areas
- Sales Performance
Tracking revenue, order volume, and average order value over time.
- Customer Insights
Studying demographics, purchase frequency, and retention rates.
- Product Analysis
Identifying best‑selling items, underperforming products, and seasonal demand.
- Channel Effectiveness
Comparing sales across platforms (website, mobile app, marketplaces).
- Marketing Impact
Measuring how campaigns, discounts, and ads influence sales.
- Geographic Trends
Understanding which regions contribute most to revenue.
  Purpose
- Optimize pricing, promotions, and inventory.
- Improve customer experience and loyalty.
- Forecast future sales and plan resources.
- Increase profitability by focusing on high‑value products and customers.
   Typical Tools & Methods
- Data Visualization: Charts and dashboards (e.g., Plotly, Tableau, Power BI).
- Statistical Analysis: Growth rates, correlation, regression.
- KPIs: Conversion rate, cart abandonment rate, repeat purchase rate.
